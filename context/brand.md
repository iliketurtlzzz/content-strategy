# MarketWake Brand Context

## About MarketWake
MarketWake is a full-service digital marketing agency based in Atlanta, GA. We help businesses grow through strategic digital marketing — from SEO and paid media to web development and creative services.

## Brand Voice

### Tone Attributes
- **Confident, not arrogant** — We know our stuff and prove it with results
- **Direct, not blunt** — Get to the point without being cold
- **Smart, not academic** — Expertise without jargon overload
- **Energetic, not hype-y** — Enthusiasm backed by substance
- **Approachable, not casual** — Professional but human

### On-Voice Examples
- "We increased organic traffic 340% in 6 months. Here's exactly how."
- "Most agencies guess. We measure, test, and iterate until the numbers tell the story."
- "Your website isn't a brochure — it's your hardest-working salesperson."

### Off-Voice Examples (Never Do This)
- "In today's ever-changing digital landscape..." (generic filler)
- "We leverage synergies to optimize your digital footprint" (jargon soup)
- "HUGE ANNOUNCEMENT!!!!" (hype without substance)
- "We're passionate about helping brands succeed" (empty claim)

## Core Services
1. **SEO & Content Marketing** — Technical SEO, content strategy, link building
2. **Paid Media (PPC/SEM)** — Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, programmatic
3. **Web Design & Development** — Custom WordPress, Shopify, performance optimization
4. **Social Media Marketing** — Strategy, content creation, community management
5. **Email Marketing** — Campaign strategy, automation, list management
6. **Analytics & Reporting** — GA4, dashboards, attribution modeling
7. **Creative Services** — Brand identity, graphic design, video production

## Differentiators
- Data-driven approach with transparent reporting
- Atlanta-based with national client roster
- Full-service under one roof (no outsourced piecework)
- Senior-level strategists on every account (not junior staff learning on your dime)
- Results-focused — we tie everything back to revenue impact

## Brand Guidelines
- Primary color: Reference MarketWake brand kit
- Logo usage: Always use approved assets
- Photography: Authentic, energetic, diverse — no stock photo cliches

## Competitive Positioning
We sit between boutique specialists (great at one thing) and big holding-company agencies (expensive, slow, impersonal). MarketWake offers enterprise-quality strategy with the speed and attention of a focused team.
