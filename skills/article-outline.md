# Skill: Article Outline

## Purpose
Create a strategic outline for any article before writing full content.

## When to Use
- User asks to write a blog post, guide, or article
- Topic is complex and needs structural planning
- Content needs SEO alignment before drafting

## Procedure

1. **Clarify the objective**: What should the reader know/do/feel after reading?
2. **Identify the persona**: Check `profiles/` — who is this for?
3. **Map the pillar**: Education, Authority, Awareness, or Conversion?
4. **Research angle**: What's the unique MarketWake perspective? (Not just "what is X")
5. **Define primary keyword** and 3-5 secondary keywords
6. **Build the outline**:

```
# [Title — keyword-optimized, benefit-driven]

Meta Description: [150-160 chars]
Primary Keyword: [keyword]
Secondary Keywords: [list]
Pillar: [Education/Authority/Awareness/Conversion]
Persona: [target persona]
Word Count Target: [range]

## Introduction
- Hook: [specific hook approach]
- Promise: [what reader will learn/gain]

## Section 1: [H2]
- Key point
- Supporting data/example
- Subsection if needed (H3)

## Section 2: [H2]
...

## Section N: [H2]
...

## Key Takeaways
- Bullet summary of main points

## CTA
- [Specific next step tied to MW service]

## Internal Links to Include
- [Relevant MW blog posts or service pages]
```

7. **Present outline for approval** before writing full content

## Output Format
Numbered/hierarchical list with brief descriptions per section. Keep it concise and adjustable.
