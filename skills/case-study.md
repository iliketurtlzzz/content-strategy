# Skill: Case Study Creation

## Purpose
Transform client results into compelling case studies that prove MarketWake's value.

## When to Use
- Client achieves significant results worth showcasing
- Sales team needs proof points for a specific vertical
- Authority pillar content needed

## Procedure

1. **Gather inputs** (ask user for):
   - Client name (or anonymized descriptor)
   - Industry/vertical
   - Services provided
   - Timeline
   - Key metrics (before/after)
   - Challenges faced
   - Any client quotes

2. **Write using the framework**:

```
# [Result-Driven Title: "How [Client/Industry] Achieved [Specific Result] in [Timeframe]"]

## The Challenge
- What problem did the client face?
- What had they tried before?
- What was at stake?
[2-3 paragraphs, paint the picture]

## The Strategy
- What did MarketWake identify as the opportunity?
- What approach did we recommend and why?
- What made this different from generic solutions?
[2-4 paragraphs, show strategic thinking]

## The Execution
- What specific tactics were deployed?
- What tools/platforms were used?
- What did the timeline look like?
[2-3 paragraphs with specifics]

## The Results
- Key metrics with before/after comparison
- Use a clear data visualization format:
  | Metric | Before | After | Change |
  |--------|--------|-------|--------|
  | [metric] | [value] | [value] | [+/- %] |

- Quote from client (if available)

## Key Takeaways
- What can other businesses learn from this?
- What made the difference?

## CTA
- "Facing a similar challenge? [Let's talk about your growth strategy.]"
```

3. **Create derivative content**:
   - LinkedIn summary post
   - Key stat graphic concept
   - Email highlight for newsletter
   - Sales one-pager bullet points

## Quality Checks
- All numbers accurate and approved by client
- No confidential information without permission
- Results are honestly represented (no cherry-picking)
- Client has approved use of their name/brand (if named)
