# Skill: Email Sequence Creation

## Purpose
Create strategic email campaigns and automated sequences for MarketWake or MW clients.

## When to Use
- Newsletter content needed
- Lead nurture sequence required
- Campaign promotion emails
- Onboarding or re-engagement sequences

## Procedure

1. **Define the sequence**:
   - Goal (nurture, convert, retain, re-engage)
   - Audience persona
   - Number of emails
   - Timing/cadence
   - Trigger (if automated)

2. **Write each email**:

```
---
Email [#] of [Total]
Send Timing: [Day X / trigger + delay]
Subject Line: [6-10 words, curiosity or benefit-driven]
Preview Text: [Complement, don't repeat subject]
---

[Opening — personal, direct, connect to their situation]

[Body — one core message, 150-300 words]
- Lead with value
- One idea per paragraph
- Specific examples or data

[CTA — single, clear action]
[Button text]: [action-oriented, specific]

[Sign-off — from a real person on the MW team]
```

3. **Sequence architecture**:

### Lead Nurture (5-email example)
| Email | Timing | Purpose | CTA |
|-------|--------|---------|-----|
| 1 | Immediate | Welcome + deliver lead magnet | Read/download |
| 2 | Day 3 | Quick win / value add | Try this tip |
| 3 | Day 7 | Social proof / case study | See results |
| 4 | Day 14 | Address objection | Learn more |
| 5 | Day 21 | Direct offer | Book a call |

4. **Deliver with metadata**:
```
Sequence Name:
Goal:
Persona:
Emails: [count]
Cadence:
Pillar:
Subject Lines: [all listed]
```

## Standards
- Subject lines: Test 2-3 variants per email
- One primary CTA per email (secondary link OK in PS)
- Mobile-friendly formatting (short paragraphs, clear buttons)
- Unsubscribe language compliant with CAN-SPAM
- Personal tone — from a human, not "the MarketWake team"
