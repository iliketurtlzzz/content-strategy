# Skill: Content Calendar Planning

## Purpose
Plan a strategic content calendar that aligns with MarketWake's pillar distribution and business goals.

## When to Use
- Monthly or weekly content planning
- Quarterly content strategy sessions
- Campaign planning around events/launches

## Procedure

1. **Gather inputs**:
   - Time period (week/month/quarter)
   - Key dates (holidays, events, launches, client milestones)
   - Priority services to promote
   - Any trending topics from trend-researcher agent
   - Existing content to repurpose

2. **Apply pillar distribution**:
   - Education: 40% of content
   - Authority: 30% of content
   - Awareness: 20% of content
   - Conversion: 10% of content

3. **Map to cadence targets**:
   - Blog: 2-4 posts/month
   - LinkedIn: 3-5 posts/week
   - Instagram: 3-4 posts/week
   - Email: 1-2/month
   - Case studies: 1/month
   - X/Twitter: Daily

4. **Build the calendar**:

```
# Content Calendar: [Month Year]

## Theme: [Monthly focus area]

### Week 1: [Date Range]
| Day | Platform | Pillar | Topic | Format | Persona | Status |
|-----|----------|--------|-------|--------|---------|--------|
| Mon | LinkedIn | Education | [topic] | Post | [persona] | Draft |
| Tue | Instagram | Authority | [topic] | Carousel | [persona] | Draft |
| Wed | Blog | Education | [topic] | Article | [persona] | Outline |
| Thu | LinkedIn | Awareness | [topic] | Post | [persona] | Draft |
| Fri | X/Twitter | Education | [topic] | Thread | [persona] | Draft |

### Week 2: [Date Range]
...

## Email Schedule
| Date | Type | Subject | Persona | Pillar |
|------|------|---------|---------|--------|
| [date] | Newsletter | [subject] | [persona] | [pillar] |

## Key Dates This Month
- [Date]: [Event/holiday/launch]

## Repurpose Opportunities
- [Blog post] → [LinkedIn carousel] + [Email highlight] + [Twitter thread]
```

5. **Review checklist**:
   - [ ] Pillar distribution roughly matches targets
   - [ ] All personas represented
   - [ ] Mix of formats (not all text posts)
   - [ ] Key dates/events covered
   - [ ] Repurpose opportunities identified
   - [ ] CTAs varied (not always "book a call")
